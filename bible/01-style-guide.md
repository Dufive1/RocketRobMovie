# Rocket Rob — Style Guide

## Animation Style

**Pixar-inspired stylized character design.** Every element of Rocket Rob's visual world follows the principles that make Pixar and modern DreamWorks characters instantly appealing and emotionally readable.

### Key Traits

- Large expressive eyes
- Rounded facial features
- Slightly oversized heads
- Clean, smooth surfaces
- Bright, saturated colors
- Friendly heroic proportions

### Reference Touchstones

Think: Pixar, DreamWorks, modern animated film character sheets.

NOT: Realistic proportions, comic-book gritty realism, adult superhero anatomy.

## The Rule

**All Rocket Rob images, environments, and characters must follow Pixar character rules:**

- Stylized proportions
- Soft edges
- Clean lighting
- Expressive faces
- Friendly tone
- No realistic anatomy

This applies to every visual produced for the film and any subsequent expansion — character art, environments, props, marketing materials, merchandise.

## Character Proportions

### Standard (Rob)

- **Height ratio**: 7 heads tall
- Slightly oversized head
- Compact torso
- Expressive hands (important for dad gestures — pointing, facepalming, hugging)
- Athletic but not bulky — capable, not a bodybuilder
- Overall: "A dad who accidentally became a superhero"

### Child (Max)

- **Height ratio**: 4–5 heads tall
- Even larger head-to-body ratio than adults
- Huge expressive eyes
- Slightly oversized hockey gear (the gear wears him, not the other way around)
- Bouncy, energetic posing

### Adult Female (Claire)

- **Height ratio**: 6.5–7 heads tall
- Elegant but warm proportions
- Confident posture
- Expressive eyes and eyebrows (matches Rob's expressiveness)

## Typography

### Logo

The Rocket Rob logo uses bold, rounded letterforms with gold trim and a shield shape incorporating the RR monogram. The maple leaf accent ties to Rob's Canadian heritage. See: `art/branding/logo-explorations.png`

### In-Universe Text

All in-universe signage, titles, and text should use rounded, friendly typefaces. Nothing sharp, gothic, or aggressive.

## Environment Style

### Architecture

- Rounded Pixar shapes
- Soft lighting with warm color temperature
- Bright color accents against neutral bases
- Modern but not cold
- "Pixar + Apple aesthetic" — clean, warm, cinematic

### Lighting

- Soft diffused key lighting for daytime scenes
- Warm golden hour for emotional moments
- Rich saturated nighttime (never muddy or desaturated, even during villain attacks)
- The city always feels optimistic, even in danger

### Key Locations

| Location | Visual Character |

| Titan Sports Tech | Sleek glass tower, cool blues, corporate clean |
| Max's Hockey Arena | Small, warm, community-scale, bright ice |
| The City Skyline | Signature bridge, Titan tower as anchor, rounded rooftops |

## Mood and Tone in Art

Every piece of Rocket Rob art should visually communicate:

- **Optimism** — Even in conflict, the palette stays warm
- **Family** — Characters are often physically close, touching, connected
- **Imagination** — Max's fantasy sequences are more saturated and dynamic than "reality"
- **Humor** — Poses should be slightly exaggerated, expressive, alive
